About Us

VyrJion Studios® is a collaborative development network focused on building high-quality, innovation-driven games rooted in passion projects and long-term creative vision.

The studio originally began as Spectrum Studios, a solo development project centered around its primary title, Dragon Ball Spectrum. On September 2nd, 2025, the studio rebranded into VyrJion Studios® / Network, establishing a co-chairman system between Jozef Archām and the founder, Jan.

Today, our focus has expanded beyond its original project. Our mission is now centered on producing high-quality games with strong innovation, structure, and creative consistency across all projects.
